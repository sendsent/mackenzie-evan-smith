Mackenzie Evan Smith Sajan


My writing has appeared in ZYZZYVA, Crab Orchard Review, and TriQuarterly Online and has been awarded grants and fellowships from Hedgebrook, Hawthornden Castle, the Elizabeth George Foundation, and the U.S. Fulbright Commission, the Henry Luce Foundation, and the U.S. State Department. I have taught creative writing workshops in Belize, India, Montenegro, and Timor-Leste and for incarcerated writers in California and Oregon. I hold a BA from Carnegie Mellon University and an MFA from Oregon State University and live in Oregon where I run a science communications office at a UC Berkeley research institute.
